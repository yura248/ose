OUR TECHNOLOGY

ACTIVE NOISE CANCELLING

Active Noise Cancellation (ANC) uses noise-cancelling speakers to reduce unwanted background noise.
Ose utilise advanced active noise cancelling technology that continuously pinpoints and measures ambient noise
in order to block out the things you don’t want to hear. Now you can focus on what matters most – the music.
Whether you prefer complete immersion with noise-cancellation, a straightforward wired connection
or the total freedom of true wireless, we’ve got your back.
