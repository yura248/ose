FAQ

  • HOW CAN I STREAM MUSIC TO MY OSE SPEAKERS?
    To stream music to your speakers, use your favourite streaming app from your phone or tablet while connected to your Wi-Fi network.
    Depending on your product, you have three streaming options:
    - Ose multiroom: requires the Ose App.
    - AirPlay: you can activate it by selecting the AirPlay symbol on your phone or streaming app.
    - Chromecast: you can activate it by selecting the Chromecast symbol on your phone or streaming app (requires the Google Home App).
  • HOW CAN I ADJUST THE SOUND PROFILE OF MY SPEAKERS?
    To adjust your speaker's sound modes, start the Ose App and select your speaker.
    In the listening modes section, go to the Beosonic equalizer and move the circle to the audio settings you prefer: warm, bright, relaxed or energetic.
    You can save the settings as personal listening modes for easy access next time you activate the speaker.
  • GENERAL SHIPPING INFORMATION

    Once the warehouse has been able to verify that we have received the package back, a credit will be made to your account within 14 days.
